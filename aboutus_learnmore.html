Community we serve

We serve various categories of patients from various different backgrounds – both in OPD and IPD. West Bengal Government Employees under WBGHS – both OPD and IPD. Central Government Employees under CGHS – only in OPD. Here we do not offer IPD service due to limited Bed Capacity. We cater to all insurance companies and TPA and offer cashless insurance facilities. We have a large number of corporate clients for both OPD and IPD services. We cater to Kolkata Police.
